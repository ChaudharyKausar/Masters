Struggling With Admissions? Try Digital Marketing

  • John Doe
  • Jan 1, 2022
  • 12 Comments

Currently, we are seeing many schools, colleges, and educational institutions facing drops in enrollments while budgeting heavily for promotional material. Parents and students are searching online and investigating before choosing an institute. Supply teachers and give out leaflets, community posters and word of mouth just won't do the job anymore. If an educational institute doesn't have a solid and effective digital marketing strategy it is an instant credibility and visibility loss. From SEO for schools and colleges to engaging social campaigns, an educational marketing strategy can make a difference in getting enrollments. This blog will examine the drop off in enrollments and why an Education Digital Marketing strategy can make an impact for an institution.

Why Educational Institutes Fail at Admissions?

The education industry has become extremely competitive and when parents are looking into the institution before applying, they compare everything. Often, the institutions that do not succeed at education marketing make similar mistakes. They have outdated websites, no education SEO, or lack of online visibility. Today's students want interactions in the content they consume, including virtual tours for education, success stories of alumni, or engaging with students through social media for schools experiences. If education is not being marketed through a digital platform, all colleges, despite being reputable, will lose applications to institutions with a stronger online presence.

Mistakes Schools Make in Marketing

Here are some of the most common reasons that your admissions campaigns could be falling flat:

  • Lack of an online presence: Parents can’t find you online when they search on Google.
  • Poor SEO for schools and colleges: If your school or college won’t rank for things like “best school in [city]”, you won’t get any inquiries.
  • Using generic ads: Marketing that tries to fit everyone’s needs will never match parents' true priorities.
  • Not using content marketing for education well: No stories of success, no highlights of your curriculum, and no evidence about educators you have teaching lowers trustworthiness.
  • Ignoring reviews: Negative reviews that are left unmanaged can ultimately destroy credibility.

These missteps will all lead to a large gap between parent expectations and the output of the institutes marketing.

Digital Marketing Solutions for Admissions Growth

Benefits of Digital Marketing for Schools and Colleges to Drive Student Admissions:

  • Education SEO: Optimize for local descriptive search terms. For example, “schools ICSE near me” or “MBA college Mumbai”.
  • Google Ads for education: Implement advertising campaigns on Google Ads that are targeted at parents and students actively searching for courses.
  • Social media campaigns Communicate student success and achievement and provide images to entice parents and students to experience a glimpse of campus life.
  • Virtual tours & webinars: Provide visiting prospective students with the opportunity to experience campus life before they visit in person.
  • Content marketing for education: Building authority, trust, and perspective can be achieved through informative blogs, career guides, and video testimonials from students.
  • WhatsApp campaigns for schools: Quickly communicate admissions reminders, event invitations, and parental communication without fuss.

All of these initiatives will provide schools and colleges the opportunity to engage with the correct target audience, build trust and credibility, and convert enquiries into student admissions.
